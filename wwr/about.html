Row or Die Rafting

We turn river chaos into core memories. Whether you're here to conquer Class V rapids or just survive family bonding, our certified guides guarantee adrenaline, safety, and post-trip bragging rights. No passengers – only paddle warriors.

History

Born from a 2015 bet between five river-obsessed rebels, Row or Die started with one rusty raft and zero common sense. Today, we’ve flipped over 2,000 tourists (safely), served 10,000 ‘Birria Beast’ tacos, and turned karaoke nights into extreme sports. The rapids? Still winning. The stories? Unforgettable.
