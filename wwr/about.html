White Water Rafting

Whether you're here to conquer Class V rapids or just survive family bonding, our certified guides guarantee adrenaline, safety, and post-trip bragging rights. No passengers—only paddle warriors.

History

Born from a 2015 bet between five river-obsessed rebels, Row or Die started with one rusty raft and zero common sense. Today, we’ve flipped over 2,000 tourists (safely) and turned karaoke nights into extreme sports.

We grew by focusing on safety, skilled guides, and unforgettable stories on every trip. The rapids keep winning. The stories? Unforgettable.
