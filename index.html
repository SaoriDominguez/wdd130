Mexico

Mexico is a colorful and vibrant country, rich in culture, history, and traditions. Its geographical diversity is impressive: it has beaches, jungles, deserts, mountains, and modern cities. Its delicious and diverse gastronomy is recognized as Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity by UNESCO. From ancient ruins to modern cities and beautiful flora and fauna, Mexico offers an unforgettable experience to all who visit.
